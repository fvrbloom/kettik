"Myn Bala"

The undoubted success of Kazakhfilm in staging historical paintings on the theme of the war of the Dzungars and the Kazakhs.The film not only has good drama, but also technically it looks great: great battle scenes, new young faces, a lot of dynamics in action. As a result, the picture received a lot of positive reviews not only at home, but also abroad.

"A Gift to Stalin"

The historical drama "A Gift to Stalin" tells about 1949: a new wave of repression and the 70th anniversary of Stalin through the eyes of a Jewish boy Sasha. The film reveals the events of August 29, 1949, when the first test of the Soviet atomic bomb was carried out in the Family and this incident took the lives of Sasha's loved ones.

WATCH NOW

"Kyz Zhibek"

The film "Kyz-Zhibek" is based on the poem of the same name about tragic love, which is one of the masterpieces of traditional Kazakh folklore. This classic story was first published at the end of the 19th century and quickly became a source of inspiration for many artists. The legend of Kyz Zhibek was the basis for the opera of the same name by E.G. Brusilovsky, and a ballet was also staged.

Things to do in Kazakhstan to learn its culture

VISIT ETHNO AUL

Ethno aul is a real opportunity to travel to the past and feel yourself in the image of a real nomad, to feel the atmosphere of centuries gone by. The national cultural complex will introduce visitors to the rich history, culture, art and traditions of the Kazakh people, where anyone will be transported to the ethno-cultural environment of nomadic civilization and fully enjoy the daily and festive life of the village.

HOLD A GOLDEN EAGLE

A good way to feel like a nomad is to hold a tamed golden eagle, imagine yourself for a moment as a nomad hunter. Hunting for nomads has always been an aid to cattle breeding, had a great educational value. We hunted with hunting birds, with greyhounds.

TRY NATIONAL DISHES

Kazakh national food reflects the traditional nomadic lifestyle. And if you are planning to visit Kazakhstan, be prepared to see a wide range of meat dishes. Some dishes may appear extraordinary or even strange, but everything is worth trying at least once.
